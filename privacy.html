Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 February 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Wordle Six without signing in, we do not collect any personal information. Game data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage.

When you sign in using a third-party provider (GitHub, Discord, or Google), we collect:

  • Provider user ID — a unique identifier from your authentication provider
  • Display name — your public username or name from the provider
  • Avatar URL — your profile picture URL from the provider

Additionally, with each game interaction we collect:

  • IP address — used for anti-cheat verification via IP geolocation
  • Timezone offset — your browser's UTC offset, used to detect timezone manipulation

We do not collect or store your email address, password, or any other personal information beyond the above.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Identify your account and display your name on the leaderboard
  • Sync your game progress and statistics across devices
  • Display your avatar in the user interface

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in a SQLite database on a self-hosted server. We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. There are no cookies used for tracking — only a session cookie for authentication.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is only visible to you and, where applicable, on the public leaderboard (display name and game statistics only).

5. Third-Party Authentication

When you sign in, you are redirected to your chosen provider (GitHub, Discord, or Google) to authorise access. We only receive the information listed in Section 1. Please review the privacy policies of these providers for details on how they handle your data.

6. Anti-Cheat Monitoring

We monitor for timezone manipulation and other forms of cheating to maintain fair competition on the leaderboard. IP addresses and timezone data are logged when suspicious activity is detected. Suspicious activity may result in account suspension per the Terms of Service.

7. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists. If you wish to have your data deleted, please contact the operator via GitHub.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encrypted sessions (HTTPS) and secure authentication tokens. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please reach out via GitHub.
